About ASA ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN

ASA Architects is a professional practice with a wide range of experience in architectural and urban designs projects. The company was established in 1995 and is currently based in Hyde Park, Johannesburg, South Africa.

Our focus is on urban design, large residential and mixed-use developments and Inner City regeneration projects. These include government projects, as well as private clients. As such, projects are conducted to meet specific objectives to ensure they meet social development goals.

  • Urban Design

    These services provide integrated spatial development frameworks and strategies, from broad regional conceptual planning to precinct planning and design guidelines.

  • ARCHITECTURAL AND INTERIOR DESIGN SERVICES

    We offer full architectural and interior design services, from initial conceptual planning to detailed design, contract documentation and supervision of works.

  • SUPPLEMENTARY SERVICES

    Feasibility studies developed to the point where the Client is satisfied that quality objectives can be achieved within their time and cost parameters.

  • PROJECT MANAGEMENT SERVICES

    In our capacity as Principal Agents we provide project management and project administration services. At the end of each project we put in place a manual that will assist in further maintenance and management of developments.

  • Our Mission Statement

    We are committed to the process of design and planning as a team effort together with the client and the team of professionals. As a result, an excellent ratio of repeat business from our clients bears testimony to the consistent quality of our services. In the field of social and affordable housing we are committed, and succeeded so far, to produce designs that look and feel like developments in any location and for any income group. Our designs revolve always around the creation of quality, sustainable environments, by reducing as much as possible the carbon footprint, through design as well as through construction process.
